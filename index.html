My Littile Brother (Character Clay Model)

2020, clay, 12.5x4x4cm

Clay sculpture based on character design.

It tells a family story about a girl and her brother. It discusses how two children should deal with each other's relationship in the family through this story, which is also inspired by my brother and me. It is inevitable that there are conflicts between two children. We should put aside the prejudice of age and learn to apologize voluntarily.
